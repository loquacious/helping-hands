About the Charity

The Color Vibe 5K Run is all abaout having fun and helping out local charities in your area. The admission money for the race will go to local charities like the Greenville Humaine Society, Greenville Miracle Hill, Meals on wheels, etc. We as an organization try to be the middle man between serving the local organizations in the area and having a blast at the same time. Go to our website to learn more about what we do.

About the Event

The Color Vibe 5k is all about having fun and helping people at the same time. When you sign up you will get a t-shirt and a pair of glasses. This year the event will be held at the Swamp Rabbit Trial near Clemson Univerity. You will get blown with color chalk at the beginning and throughout the race. At the end we will have a blast with a huge color pep rally. The donation that you make will aid local charity organizations. Our hope by the end of the day is that you will have a blast and we can help others in their need. Sign up now and come run with us. Go to our website to learn more about what we do.
